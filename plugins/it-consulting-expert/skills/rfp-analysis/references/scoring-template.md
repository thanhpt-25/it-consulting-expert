# Go/No-Go Scoring Template (案件評価テンプレート)

## Dimension Scoring Guide

### Strategic Fit (戦略適合性) — Weight: 15%
| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 5 | Target industry, target account, strong follow-on potential, board-level visibility |
| 4 | Target industry OR target account, good follow-on potential |
| 3 | Adjacent industry, moderate strategic value |
| 2 | Outside core focus, limited strategic value |
| 1 | Misaligned with strategy, could dilute brand positioning |

### Capability Match (技術適合性) — Weight: 25%
| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 5 | Deep expertise in all required technologies, strong past performance in similar projects |
| 4 | Strong in most areas, minor gaps fillable through partners |
| 3 | Capable but stretching — need to upskill or partner significantly |
| 2 | Major capability gaps, heavy reliance on subcontractors |
| 1 | Fundamental mismatch — we don't do this type of work |

### Win Probability (受注確度) — Weight: 25%
| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 5 | Strong client relationship, no incumbent, our differentiators match evaluation criteria |
| 4 | Good relationship OR strong technical fit, favorable evaluation criteria |
| 3 | Level playing field, no clear advantage or disadvantage |
| 2 | Incumbent present, or evaluation criteria favor competitor strengths |
| 1 | Appears wired for another vendor, or we're an unknown to the client |

### Profitability (収益性) — Weight: 15%
| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 5 | Large deal, healthy margins (>25%), favorable payment terms |
| 4 | Good deal size, acceptable margins (15-25%) |
| 3 | Moderate deal, thin margins (10-15%), standard terms |
| 2 | Small deal relative to effort, or margin risk from fixed-price with unclear scope |
| 1 | Likely unprofitable, or onerous penalty/liability terms |

### Delivery Risk (デリバリーリスク) — Weight: 10%
| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 5 | Clear requirements, realistic timeline, proven technology, simple integrations |
| 4 | Mostly clear, minor ambiguities, manageable complexity |
| 3 | Some ambiguity, moderately tight timeline, some complex integrations |
| 2 | Vague requirements, aggressive timeline, or significant technical unknowns |
| 1 | Requirements unclear, impossible timeline, high-risk integrations, regulatory landmines |

### Resource Availability (リソース確保) — Weight: 10%
| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 5 | Key roles available on bench, full team assembable within 2 weeks |
| 4 | Most key roles available, 1-2 need recruiting/partnering |
| 3 | PM or TL available, but need significant hiring or subcontracting |
| 2 | Key roles require new hires, timeline to staff is tight |
| 1 | Cannot staff critical roles, or conflicts with committed projects |

## Red Flag Checklist

Check each — any "Yes" should trigger deeper discussion:

- [ ] Submission deadline < 2 weeks away (not enough time for quality proposal)
- [ ] Budget stated is < 50% of our rough estimate
- [ ] Uncapped penalty clauses or liquidated damages
- [ ] Mandatory certifications we don't hold and can't obtain in time
- [ ] Client has history of canceling projects or non-payment
- [ ] RFP evaluation criteria weight "price" at > 50% (pure price competition)
- [ ] Scope includes technologies we've never used in production
- [ ] Timeline requires go-live before we could realistically complete testing
- [ ] RFP was issued to > 10 vendors (diluted win probability)
- [ ] Incumbent vendor wrote the technical requirements (wired RFP signals)
