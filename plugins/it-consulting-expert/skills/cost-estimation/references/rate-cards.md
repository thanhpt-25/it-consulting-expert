## Pricing Model Guidelines

### Fixed Price (一括請負)
- Calculate: (Total Man-Months × Blended Rate) + Non-Labor + Buffer
- **Blended rate**: weighted average across all roles
- **Risk premium**: add 15-25% depending on scope clarity
- **Payment schedule options**:
  - Equal installments: 33-33-34 or 25-25-25-25
  - Milestone-based: 30 (kickoff) - 30 (mid) - 30 (delivery) - 10 (acceptance)
  - Progressive: 20-20-30-20-10

### Time & Materials (準委任)
- Bill actual time at agreed role-based rates
- Set monthly budget cap (上限) if client requires
- Include minimum commitment period (typically 3 months)
- Termination notice: typically 30-60 days

### Hybrid
- Fixed price for well-defined phases (requirements, design)
- T&M for development phase with estimated range
- Cap-and-collar: fixed price ± 10-15% adjustment range
